FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free consultation or site visit?

Yes — we provide absolutely free initial consultations and site surveys. Our engineers will assess your space, calculate cooling loads, and recommend the right HVAC system with no obligation.

How long does a typical HVAC installation take?

Timeline depends on the scope. Split AC projects typically take 1–3 days. VRF and ductable projects for medium-sized offices take 2–4 weeks. Large chiller plants may take 4–8 weeks including civil coordination.

Which brands do you work with?

We work with all major HVAC brands — Blue Star, Daikin, Mitsubishi, Voltas, LG, Samsung, Hitachi, and more. We recommend the best brand-system combination based on your budget, capacity, and application requirements.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes — we offer comprehensive AMC plans for all types of HVAC systems including chillers, VRF, ductable and split AC. Our AMC packages include quarterly service visits, priority response, and full documentation.

Do you serve locations outside Thoothukudi and Tiruchirappalli?

Yes — we serve clients across Tamil Nadu. While our primary offices are in Thoothukudi and Tiruchirappalli, our project teams travel statewide for installations. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
