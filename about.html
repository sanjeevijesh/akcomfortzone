Our Journey

10 Years of Cooling Tamil Nadu

From a bold idea in 2014 to one of Tamil Nadu's most trusted HVAC turnkey companies.

2014
November 2014 — Founded

AK Comfortzone Established

Two passionate engineers from Blue Star Ltd took the leap and founded AK Comfortzone in Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu — with a shared vision of bringing enterprise-level HVAC expertise to businesses of all sizes.

2015
2015 — First Milestone

50 Commercial Projects Completed

Within the first year, AK Comfortzone delivered over 50 successful commercial HVAC installations across Thoothukudi — spanning hotels, offices, and small industrial units.

2017
2017 — Expansion

Tiruchirappalli Office Opened

Growing demand from the central Tamil Nadu market led us to open our second office in Tiruchirappalli — extending our turnkey HVAC services to a wider geography.

2019
2019 — Major Milestone

500+ Combined Projects Delivered

A proud milestone — our two founders together crossed 500 successfully completed HVAC projects, serving clients across 12+ industry verticals from pharma to data centers.

2022
2022 — Growth

Serving 12+ Industry Verticals

AK Comfortzone expanded its service portfolio to serve over 12 distinct industry sectors across Tamil Nadu, from hospitals and pharma to IT companies, malls, and industrial plants.

2024
2024 — 10 Year Anniversary 🎉

A Decade of Cooling Tamil Nadu

Celebrating 10 years of trust, expertise, and commitment to quality — AK Comfortzone continues to be Tamil Nadu's go-to HVAC turnkey partner, with a 4.7★ rating and a growing team of certified professionals.
